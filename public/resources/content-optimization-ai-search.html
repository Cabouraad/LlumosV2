Content Types That Get Cited

  • Listicles and rankings: 25% citation rate
  • How-to guides: Step-by-step content with clear instructions
  • Data-driven research: Original statistics and benchmarks
  • Comparison content: "Best," "Top," and "vs" articles
  • FAQ content: Direct question-and-answer format

Optimization Tactics That Work

1. Structure for Extraction

AI systems extract information from well-structured content. Use clear headers, bullet points, and tables that make key information scannable.

2. Answer Questions Directly

Start sections with direct answers before elaborating. AI systems favor content that provides immediate value.

3. Include Data and Sources

Content with statistics, research citations, and verifiable data gets cited more frequently than opinion-based content.

4. Keep Content Fresh

AI systems prefer content that's 25.7% newer on average. Update your key pages quarterly at minimum.

5. Implement Schema Markup

Structured data improves AI understanding of your content by approximately 30%.

Content Refresh Checklist

  1. Update statistics and data points
  2. Add recent examples and case studies
  3. Improve structure with clear headers
  4. Include FAQ sections with common questions
  5. Add schema markup (Article, FAQ, HowTo)
  6. Remove outdated information
