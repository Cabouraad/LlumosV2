What Gets Cited in AI Overviews

Research reveals the factors that increase citation probability:

  • Brand mentions across the web: 0.664 correlation with AIO appearance
  • Schema markup: +30% citation improvement
  • Content freshness: AI prefers newer content by 25.7%
  • Backlinks: 0.218 correlation (less important than brand mentions)

Surprising Finding

40% of sources cited in AI Overviews are pages ranking lower than position 10 in traditional SERPs. This means you can win AI visibility without winning traditional rankings.

Action Plan

  • Prioritize brand mentions and PR over pure link building
  • Implement comprehensive schema markup
  • Update content quarterly to maintain freshness
  • Create content that directly answers common questions
  • Track AI visibility separately from traditional rankings
