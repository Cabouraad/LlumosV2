SaaS AI Visibility Case Study: How B2B Brands Win in AI Search

15 min read

When a mid-market project management SaaS company noticed they were absent from AI responses while competitors like Asana and Monday.com were consistently mentioned, they knew they had a visibility problem.

The Challenge

Despite having a competitive product and strong traditional SEO, this SaaS company was invisible in AI search. When potential customers asked ChatGPT or Perplexity for project management recommendations, competitors appeared—but they didn't.

12%

0.2x

The Strategy

  1. Content overhaul: Created 15 comparison pages covering every major competitor
  2. G2 optimization: Increased reviews from 120 to 400+ through customer success campaigns
  3. Thought leadership: CEO published 6 guest posts on industry publications
  4. Technical SEO: Implemented comprehensive schema markup across all product pages
  5. Reddit strategy: Team members provided genuine help in r/projectmanagement

The Results (6 Months)

Key Takeaways

  • AI visibility requires a dedicated strategy—not just good traditional SEO
  • Third-party platforms (G2, Reddit) heavily influence AI recommendations
  • Comparison content is essential for capturing "vs" and "alternative" queries
  • Consistent measurement enables strategic optimization
